Ingredients


1) One Cup : Heavy Whipping Cream
2) Two tablespoon : Chocolate syrup
3) One table spoon : Sugar
4) You can use chocolate chips /cocoa powder to decorate on top.

Procedure

Firstly in one cup of whipping cream add sugar then whip the cream. Add chocolate syrup and mix in gently. Then decorate on the top with cocoa powder/syrup /chocolate chips. Keep it in the refrigerate for an hour or two and serve it as a desert.
