Ingredients


1.maida( plain flour)
2. Sugar
3.baking powder
4. Ghee( clarified butter)
5. Curd
6.water
7. Saffron
8. Almonds ( for garnishing)

Procedure

1.first of all add maida in an bowl and then add baking powder (1 tbsp) then mix it well. The crumble texture is formed then add ghee in it and then add sugar ( half tbsp) then combine it well . Add curd in it and combine it well. Do not knead the dough but combined it and make a soft dough of it and cover it with a cloth and rest it for 15 minutes. Now for sugar syrup take another pan add water ( 1 cup) then add sugar in it and boil it till the sugarÂ  gets dissolves.then add saffron in it and boil the water for 5 minutes and the sugar syrup is ready keep it aside. Now make the small ball sized dough and make a dent at center and then place all that balls in kadai in hot oil.stir occasionally . Stir it until it becomes golden and crispy and then drop it in sugar syrup . Balushahi is ready now! Garnish the balushahi with almonds and serve it!
