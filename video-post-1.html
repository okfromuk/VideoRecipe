Ingredients


1. Mango pulp
2. Sugar
3. Ghee
4. Milk
5. Milk powder
6. Saffron

Procedure

Add ghee 1 tbsp in an pan then add (1/4) cup of Mango pulp also add saffron in it and then stir it well then add Mango pulp and sugar in it. Stir it well until the sugar dissolves and then stir it until the smooth paste forms. After 10 minutes stirring the smooth paste is formed .now transfer the batter and spread it slightly on a plate . Cool it for 5 minutes and then make small ball sized peda and create a design over it and garnish it with cashew or almonds . Mango-keshar peda is ready!
